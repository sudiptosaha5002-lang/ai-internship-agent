PRIVACY POLICY

How the Internship Finder AI Agent collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

Last Updated: April 23, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to the Internship Finder AI Agent ("the Platform", "we", "our", or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal data when you use our Smart-Powered career preparation platform.

By registering, uploading your resume, or using any feature of this Platform, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please refrain from using the Platform.

This platform is a student project built for academic and demonstration purposes. It is not a commercial service.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide and improve our services:

  • Account Information: Your full name, email address, and hashed password provided during registration.
  • Resume Data: PDF resume files uploaded by you. We parse these to extract skills, experience, and role information.
  • Extracted Profile Data: Skills, inferred job roles, years of experience, and career highlights derived from your resume using AI models.
  • Session Identifiers: A unique session_id (UUID) is generated upon upload to associate your resume data across features without requiring repeated uploads.
  • Mock Interview Responses: Answers, proctoring logs, and violation flags generated during your mock interview sessions.
  • Usage Data: General interaction data (e.g., which features you use) for improving the system. No IP addresses or device fingerprints are stored.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used exclusively to provide the features of this Platform:

  • Resume Analysis: To extract skills, calculate ATS compatibility scores, and identify skill gaps relative to market demand.
  • Internship Matching: To search and rank live job listings that align with your extracted skills and target role.
  • Mock Interview Generation: To dynamically generate technical and behavioral interview questions tailored to your profile.
  • Dashboard Personalization: To populate your dashboard with your name, role, skills, and matched opportunities.
  • AI Model Prompts: Portions of your resume text and skills are sent to third-party AI APIs (such as Google Gemini) as part of generating recommendations. These are governed by the respective provider's privacy policies.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling for third parties, or any purpose beyond what is described above.

4. Data Storage & Security

All data is stored locally on the server hosting this application. Specifically:

  • Resume Files: Stored as .pdf files in the /uploads/ directory, referenced by your unique session ID.
  • User Records: Stored in a local JSON file (users.json). Passwords are stored as SHA-256 hashed values — never in plain text.
  • Authentication Tokens: Randomly generated hex tokens used for session verification.

We implement the following security practices:

  • Password hashing using SHA-256 before storage.
  • CORS (Cross-Origin Resource Sharing) policies are enforced via Flask-CORS.
  • File upload size is limited to 32 MB to prevent abuse.
  • Only PDF files are accepted for resume upload.
As this is a local/demo project, data is not encrypted at rest. Do not upload sensitive personal documents if you have concerns about local server security.

5. Third-Party Services

This Platform integrates with the following external services. Please review their respective privacy policies:

  • Google Gemini API: Used to generate skill analysis, interview questions, and AI agent responses. Data sent includes resume text and extracted skills. See Google Privacy Policy.
  • LangChain / LangGraph: Framework used to orchestrate AI agent workflows. No data is independently stored by these libraries.
  • SerpAPI / Search Tools: Used to scrape live internship listings based on your skills. Only skill keywords are sent, not personal details.
  • Google Fonts: Used to load the Inter and Rajdhani typefaces. Google may log the font request. See Google Privacy Policy.

6. Data Retention

Data is retained for as long as the application is running on the local server. Specifically:

  • Your uploaded resume PDF is stored until manually deleted from the server's /uploads/ folder.
  • Your user profile (name, email, hashed password, skills) is retained in users.json indefinitely until deleted.
  • Mock interview session data exists only in memory during the session and is not permanently stored between server restarts.

You may request deletion of your data by contacting us at the email address listed in the footer.

7. Your Rights

As a user of this Platform, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to Access: You may request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Right to Deletion: You may request permanent deletion of your account and all associated data.
  • Right to Correction: You may request correction of inaccurate data stored about you.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: You may stop using the platform at any time. This does not automatically delete previously stored data.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at friendsgamingproject438@gmail.com.

8. Children's Privacy

This Platform is intended for use by students and professionals aged 16 years and above. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 16. If you believe a minor has registered without parental consent, please contact us immediately so we can delete the account.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the Platform after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

We recommend reviewing this page periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.
